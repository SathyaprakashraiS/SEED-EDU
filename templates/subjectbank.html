EXAMS

CBSE 12th

The AISSCE, also called Board Exams in common language, is the final examination conducted every year for high school students by the Central Board of Secondary Education on behalf of the Government of India.

CBSE 11th

General exams for class 11 to evaluate students to class 12 for future studies

CBSE 10th

All India Secondary School Examination, commonly known as board exams, is a centralized public examination that students in schools affiliated with the Central Board of Secondary Education in India take after class 10.

ACLAT

Alliance University Common Law Admission Test

AIEED

All India Entrance Examination for Design

AIIMS

All India Institute Of Medical Science, AIIMS entrance exam is for admission in any 1 of all the 6 branches of AIIMS

AILET

All India Law Entrance Test

AMU

Aligarh Muslim University Entrance Exam

BITSAT

Birla Institute of Technology and Science Admission Test

CLAT

Common Law Admission Test is a centralized national level entrance test for admissions to twenty two National Law Universities in India.

COMED-K

Consortium of Medical, Engineering and Dental Colleges of Karnataka Under Graduate Entrance Test.

CUSAT

Cochin University of Science and Technology Common Admission Test

ICAR

Indian Council of Agricultural Research

IPU-CET

Indraprastha University Common Entrance Test

JEE MAIN

Joint Entrance Examination – Main, formerly All India Engineering Entrance Examination, is an all India standardised computer-based test for admission to various undergraduate engineering, technical and architecture courses.

JEE ADVANCED

Joint Entrance Examination – Advanced, formerly the Indian Institutes of Technology-Joint Entrance Examination, is an academic examination held annually in India. It is conducted by one of the seven zonal IITs under the guidance of the Joint Admission Board.

JIPMER

The Jawaharlal Institute of Postgraduate Medical Education & Research is a medical school located at Pondicherry, the capital of the Union Territory of Puducherry, in India.

KVPY

Kishore Vaigyanik Protsahan Yojana is a scholarship programme funded by the Department of Science and Technology of the Government of India, aimed at encouraging students to take up research careers in the areas of basic sciences.

NATA

The National Aptitude Test in Architecture, is conducted by National Institute of Advanced Studies in Architecture, which is a body of Council of Architecture.

NDA

National Defence Academy Exam

NEET

The National Eligibility cum Entrance Test (Undergraduate), formerly the All India Pre-Medical Test, is an all India pre-medical entrance test for students who wish to pursue undergraduate medical.

NEST

The National Entrance Screening Test is an annual college entrance examination in India, conducted for admission into the National Institute of Science Education and Research, Bhubaneswar and the Center for Excellence in Basic Sciences, Mumbai.

NID

NID DAT is a pen paper-based exam, which is conducted in two stages for B.Des and M.Des. While Stage 1 is for Design Aptitude Test (DAT) Prelims and stage 2 is for Design Aptitude Test (DAT) Mains.

NIFT

National Institute of Fashion Technology

SAT

The SAT is a standardized test widely used for college admissions in the United States.

VITEEE

VIT Engineering Entrance Exam, an eligibility exam to join VIT
